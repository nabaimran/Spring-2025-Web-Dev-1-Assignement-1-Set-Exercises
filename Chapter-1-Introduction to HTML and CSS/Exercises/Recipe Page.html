Alfredo Chicken Pasta

Indulge in the rich and creamy delight of homemade Alfredo Chicken Pasta – a perfect balance of flavors.

Ingredients

  • 250g fettuccine pasta
  • 2 chicken breasts (sliced)
  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 3 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • Salt & pepper to taste
  • 1 tsp Italian seasoning
  • Fresh parsley (for garnish)

Method

  1. Boil the pasta in salted water until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a pan, melt butter over medium heat and cook the sliced chicken until golden brown. Remove and set aside.
  3. In the same pan, add minced garlic and sauté until fragrant.
  4. Pour in the heavy cream, Parmesan cheese, and Italian seasoning. Stir well and let it simmer.
  5. Add the cooked chicken back into the pan and mix well.
  6. Toss in the drained pasta and mix until everything is coated in the creamy sauce.
  7. Season with salt and pepper, garnish with parsley, and serve warm.

Pro Tips for the Perfect Alfredo

✨ Use fresh Parmesan cheese for the best flavor.

✨ Don't overcook the pasta; keep it al dente.

✨ Add a dash of nutmeg for an extra depth of flavor.

✨ Stir in a little pasta water if the sauce is too thick.
