📘 The Mentalist – Key Information

📺 Show Summary

The Mentalist is an American crime procedural television series that follows Patrick Jane, a former psychic medium who uses his keen observational skills to help the California Bureau of Investigation (CBI) solve crimes — while secretly hunting the serial killer Red John, who murdered his wife and daughter.

📅 Air Dates & Seasons

• Aired from: September 23, 2008 – February 18, 2015
• Total Seasons: 7
• Total Episodes: 151

🧠 Genre & Themes

Genres: Crime, Mystery, Drama, Psychological Thriller
Key Themes: Justice, revenge, deception, trauma, and moral ambiguity

🏆 Awards & Nominations

• Nominated for several People's Choice Awards and Primetime Emmys
• Simon Baker was nominated for a Golden Globe for Best Actor in a Drama Series (2009)

📍 Filming Locations

Primarily filmed in Los Angeles, California. Though set in Sacramento and across California, much of the show was shot on sound stages and backlots in Hollywood.

⏰ Episode Runtime

Each episode runs approximately 43 minutes (excluding commercials).
